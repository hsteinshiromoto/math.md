# Open Balls Are Open Sets

## Context

## Statements

**Theorem** ([[A Short Introduction to Metric Spaces|@Eck23, Theorem 1.1]]). Let be $(X,d)$ be a [[metric space]], $x\in X$, and $\varepsilon>0$. Then, the [[open ball]] $B_{<\varepsilon}(x)$ is a [[metric open set]].
