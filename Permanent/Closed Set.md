# Topology

## Context

## Statement

**Definition** ([[Tu, L. W. - An introduction to manifolds|@Tu11, pp. 319]]). Let $(X, \tau)$ be a [[topological space]] and $X_i\subset X$ be an [[open set]]. The complement $X_i^C$ is said to be a *closed set*.
